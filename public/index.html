How does it work?

Elite coaches, 1-on-1 digital training.

01

One-on-One Coaching

FYT pairs you with a remote personal trainer who has weekly video calls with you to progress you along every phase of your fitness journey. Be challenged at the convenience of your own home, a gym or at a hotel when you travel.

02

Your Custom Training Plan

Every Sunday, your coach builds a new plan based on your schedule and progress. They design guided workouts and incorporate the classes you love doing.

03

Constant Contact

Your coach checks in daily to keep you on track. Can't work out today? They will reshuffle your schedule. Traveling? They'll design quick workouts for your hotel room.
